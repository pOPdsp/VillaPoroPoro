Who We Are

Above the
treetops

Villa Poroporo is built on a hilltop in the heart of Guanacaste. The moment you step inside, you feel as if you are resting atop the forest canopy — surrounded by birdsong, mountain breezes, and breathtaking views stretching across the landscape.

It is the perfect place to rest before starting your vacation — or to recharge before heading home. A sanctuary of peace and tranquility designed to renew your energy and reconnect you with nature.

Hike along rivers and mountain trails, interact with horses on the property, watch an extraordinary variety of birds from your terrace, and enjoy the lush, exuberant views from every corner of the estate. This is pura vida — lived fully.

Experiences

Ride, explore,
feel alive

Every experience at Villa Poroporo is curated and scheduled around your stay. From horseback rides through the mountains with a hilltop picnic — greeting howler monkeys along the way — to guided hikes by rivers and trails. We organise everything so your time is simply unforgettable.

Signature Experience

Horseback Ride & Mountain Picnic

Our signature tour: ride through the mountains on horseback, greet howler monkeys in the canopy, and arrive at a scenic hilltop to enjoy a curated picnic as the sun sets over Guanacaste's golden valleys. Suitable for all levels.

$55 /person

Thermal Springs · Miravalles Volcano

Aguas Termales Guayabo

Tucked away at the base of the majestic Miravalles Volcano lies a sanctuary where time stands still. Soak in mineral-rich, golden thermal waters surrounded by tropical dry forest and the gentle whisper of volcanic steam — the perfect balance of adventure and serenity.

$30 /person

Full Day · Off-site

Rincón de la Vieja Day Trip

Full-day excursion to the active Rincón de la Vieja volcano — bubbling mud pools, natural hot springs, cascading waterfalls, and panoramic views of the Pacific coast. An experience you will never forget.

$85 /person

The Space

Life above
the canopy

Built on a hilltop, the villa puts you above the treetops — surrounded by nature, birdsong, and sweeping views.
